Celebrating Our 25th Anniversary

Dear Avalonians,

Avalon celebrates its TWENTY FIFTH anniversary during the fall/autumn of 2014. In those twenty five years the land has evolved enormously throughout its history and fundamentals remain consistent with those enjoyed by the superb players who first graced the land in the late 1980s. Funny to think, though, that many current Avalonians were not yet born when Avalon was introducing such concepts to the gaming world as player-run cities and multi-ablity skills and equilibrium and balance and guilds and Gods with followers and economies and trading and countless original innovations.

The last quarter-century has seen thousands more follow in their footsteps: brave souls venturing forth into the land to forge their own stories and emblazon themselves on Avalon's history. No game large or small has the same potential for an individual to play a defining role in the history of the entire world. Since its inception, cities have risen and flourished, suffered invasion and fallen to conquest and even complete annihilation. Deities have been struck down before the eyes of speechless mortals. Great champions have arisen -- both for good and for evil -- to contend for and attain apotheosis to the Pantheon; for no quest is greater than the battle for ORDINATION. It is an undeniable challenge to all with competitive blood in their veins, for only those of unflinching will and domination over their own mortality may walk this path of excellence; no automation or price can buy it, so priceless and unimpeachable the result of this contest fair yet greatest in difficulty. It remains renowned, beyond the shores of Avalon, for those capable of facing it with fortitude, it is undeniably the most testing challenge of any game, anywhere.

And Avalon is now FREE TO PLAY at last!

Anniversary EVENTS

The months of September and October 2014 saw the gem quests begin once again in Avalon with great festivity:

  • Clara the Parrian Thief joined the contenders by winning the Amber of the Sun in a competitive battle-sands quest run by Loki.

  • Salvador, currently a Mage and already possessor of two gems, won his third - the Topaz of Chaos - in a combat-focused quest of endurance and stamina run by Damocles.

  • The Pearl of Stars was quested by Ashvani and won by Fig of the Knights in a mixed quest testing endurance and combative skill, while Xion of the Alchemists and Julian of the Thieves won the Coral of the Sea and Opal of Compassion respectively; both demonstrating their knowledge of the land and seas in peaceful quest conditions.

  • Most recently, Threap of the Cavaliers was successful in the Guild Tournament and claimed the Ebony of Night as his prize.

This leaves but two gems remaining for competition: The Emerald of Life and the Amethyst of Time itself.

The EMERALD Gem Quest will take place over the weekend of the 8th and 9th November. It will consist of a hunt for the golden eggs of life over a 24 hour period interspersed with events throughout to give the chance at winning more eggs. It is the only Gem Quest with the freedom -- as is tradition -- for those gathering to choose to whom one's eggs are tallied.

Before the ORDINATION can take place, the list of gem-holding contenders must be whittled down in a series of sands quest eliminators until just two remain. Three of these elimination events have already taken place:

  • Clara (Thief and winner of the Amber) eliminated Fig (Knight and winner of the Pearl).

  • Julian (Thief and winner of the Opal) eliminated Xion (Alchemist and winner of the Coral).

  • Salvador (Warlock and winner of the Moonstone, Ruby and Topaz) eliminated Seraphima (Thief and winner of the Diamond).

Six contenders remain: Salvador, Threap, Plaman, Julian, Clara, Trakea. The remainder of the eliminators will take place on the weekend of 8th/9th November until just the final two remain to contest the Amethyst of time.

The competition between the last two contenders, as most know, is also the final gem quest: The AMETHYST ORDINATION. This contest involves all Avalon, fighting in desperate earnest in support of their chosen contender (and prospective deity). Whichever contender holds the Divine Amethyst at the end of the three hour event will be declared the undeniable champion to whom the prize of divinity awaits. It is an all or nothing event, the greatest and grandest in all the Avalon calendar, to be but a witness is a lifelong treasure. The winner becomes a High God for all time -- the loser gains naught.

The ORDINATION will take place on Sunday November 9th at 8PM (3PM EST). Type JOIN ORDINATION in Avalon before the event to participate or spectate! All are welcome. It is Avalon history in the making of immeasurable scale, proportion and frenetic, chaotic majesty!

Twenty Five Years and Counting

For those with a penchant for gaming history:

Avalon grew out of a prior game called Lands of the Crown (with all of four player capability!) containing just 50 of the currently 24,000 locations. This game was influenced by a superb hack'n'slasher called Mirrorworld and was started November 1988. Birthday gifts mattered in those days.

Avalon itself got its named on October 11th 1989 and was first online 28th October 1989 for a week, then went permanently online from 11th November 1989 to the general public on the IOWA and then SYNERGY systems.

Avalon is older than the internet but it first joined up with the internet through PIPEX and a clunky CISCO router (on of their first) on October 14th 1994. It was running in London at this time. A year later it shifted its base to Chicago and then soon after to a location close by the transatlantic hub by Washington DC. It has remained there ever since.

Sponsorship: Avalon Becomes Free to Play

SPONSORSHIP is absolutely FREE and bestows all the advantages of lifetime or recurring subscriptions with none of the cost. Lessons are gained at the same rate, all skills are learned the same way, all equipment open to a subscribed player is available.

There are no barriers to character development for sponsored players.

Sponsorship is obtainable via city and guild seniors or priesthood / patron of the divine orders. It is usually bestowed for between one and three months but can be extended indefinitely ensuring perpetual free access to Avalon without restriction.

City and guilds draw upon their accumulated reputation to bestow sponsorship and all regular, pro-active Avalon players earn far more reputation for their city and guild than the cost of a sponsorship.
