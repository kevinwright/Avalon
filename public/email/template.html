Avalon 25th Anniversary Newsletter

Dear Avalonians,

Avalon celebrates its TWENTY FIFTH anniversary during the month of October 2014. In those twenty five years the land has evolved enormously though its history and fundamentals remain consistent with those enjoyed by the superb players who first graced the land in the late 1980s. Funny to think, though, that many current Avalonians were not yet born when Avalon was introducing such concepts to the gaming world as player-run cities and multi-ability skills and equilibrium and balance and guilds and Gods with followers and economies and trading and countless original innovations.

The last quarter-century has seen thousands more follow in their footsteps: brave souls venturing forth into the land to forge their own stories and emblazon themselves on Avalon history. No game large or small has the same potential for an individual to play a defining role in the history of the entire world. Since its inception cities have risen and flourished, suffered invasion and fallen to conquest and even complete annihilation. Deities have been struck down before speechless mortals. Great champions have arisen - both for good and for evil - to contend through to win divinity for themselves; for no quest is greater than the battle for ORDINATION. It is an undeniable challenge to all with competitive blood in their veins and one unflinching, incontestable and unlike so many places, impossible to buy passage to excellence or automate victory against those paradigms of contendership. It remains renowned, for those capable of facing it with fortitude, the most testing and purest challenge in any game, anywhere.

Twenty Five Years and Counting

For those with a penchant for gaming history:

Avalon grew out of a prior game called Lands of the Crown (with all of FOUR player capability!) containing just 50 of the currently 24,000 locations. This game was influenced by a superb hack'n'slasher called Mirrorworld and was started November 1988. Birthday gifts mattered in those days.

Avalon itself got its named on October 11th 1989 and was first online 28th October 1989 for a week, then went permanently online from 11th November 1989 to the general public on the IOWA and then SYNERGY systems.

Avalon is older than the internet but it first joined up with the internet through PIPEX and a clunky CISCO router (on of their first) on October 14th 1994. It was running in London at this time. A year later it shifted its base to Chicago and then soon after to a location close by the transatlantic hub by Washington DC. It has remained there ever since.

Sponsorship: Avalon Becomes Free to Play

SPONSORSHIP is absolutely FREE and bestows all the advantages of lifetime or recurring subscriptions with none of the cost. Lessons are gained at the same rate, all skills are learned the same way, all equipment open to a subscribed player is available.

There are NO barriers to character development for sponsored players.

Sponsorship is obtainable via city and guild seniors or priesthood / patron of the divine orders. It is usually bestowed for between one and three months but can be extended indefinitely ensuring perpetual FREE access to Avalon without restriction.

City and guilds draw upon their accumulated reputation to bestow sponsorship and all regular, pro-active Avalon players earn far more reputation for their city and guild than the cost of a sponsorship.

If there are ANY questions about sponsorship or obtaining access to Avalon, do not hesitate to contact us at support@meetplace.net where your enquiry will be answered in a prompt and professional manner. More information can be obtained here.

Procurement Expanded: Goods and Services from the Curiosity Shop

In response to an encouraging awakening of talent both new and old, the year 1363 saw the grand opening of the Curiosity Shops across Avalon. Each city and Eleusis houses a branch of the Curiosity Shop, all offering a selection of goods and services to ease the burden of time spent gathering much-needed essentials.

A selection of the goods and services on offer:

  • 20 USD for herb pouch of 1500 lestagii (all 6 pockets with 250 lestagii)
  • 20 USD for herb pouch of essential herbs (250 herbs in each of 6 pockets)
  • 20 USD for arm-greaves, platemail, helm, shield (95% armour, max repair) plus crystal rapier (masterforged, max cared repair)
  • 25 USD for FORTY-FIVE essential potions (all 45 potions 220 sips)
  • STARTING FROM 10 USD: Gift Card of lesson-worth to be bestowed upon another player
  • 10 USD for a black cauldron of demon-summoning, complete with full runic protection and 50000 coins with which to tempt the demonic-planes
  • CUSTOM ITEMS: Unique, personalised clothing, weapon, steed or home; descriptions written and submitted or produced by the word-smiths of Olympus, supervised by Cornelius or Genesis.

Specific prices for the CUSTOM ITEMS service depend on number of items, length of description desired or number of locations (in the case of a home). Typing GOODS in the shop itself will reveal all.

  • POUCHUP: fill all pouches in your hands or worn to 250 each pocket
  • POTIONFILL: fill all potions in your inventory to 220 sips per potion
  • SECURITY: fully safeguard your inventory with oval, snowflake, anvil and anchor runes (all 90 Avalon months or three RL months protection)
  • FULLCHARGE: recharges your held/worn magical items to 1500 charges each

SERVICES are price-tailored according to one's personal inventory needs and requirements, therefore a POUCHUP for many part-filled pouches will be more costly than for just a handful of pouches well-stocked.

Where is the nearest "Curiosity Shop"?

  • In Mercinae it is NORTH from "East of Cassiandora Square"

It is important to stress that ALL of the items and services in the Curiosity Shop are equipment and acquirements of identical potency to that which EVERYONE can attain given the time and effort in-game. It is not and will never be Avalon's ethos to offer ANY purchases with real-world money that offer any advantage to a player that cannot be matched equally by time and effort made IN Avalon.
